El Clásico - FC Barcelona

Real Madrid's biggest rivalry is with FC Barcelona. The matches between these two clubs are known as "El Clásico" and are watched by millions worldwide.

Madrid Derby - Atlético Madrid

The Madrid Derby features Real Madrid against their city rivals Atlético Madrid. These matches are intense and full of passion.

El Clásico: Real Madrid vs Barcelona

The rivalry between Real Madrid and Barcelona, known as "El Clásico," is one of the most famous and intense football rivalries in the world. The matches are not just about football, but represent the political, cultural, and regional tensions between the two clubs.

The Madrid Derby: Real Madrid vs Atlético Madrid

The Madrid Derby represents the fierce competition between the two biggest clubs in Madrid. The matches are filled with passion, aggression, and high stakes, with both teams competing for dominance in the Spanish capital.

Sevilla Rivalry: Real Madrid vs Sevilla FC

Although not as globally recognized as El Clásico, the rivalry between Real Madrid and Sevilla FC has grown in importance over the years. Both teams compete for top positions in La Liga, with Sevilla often being a major threat to Madrid's title hopes.
